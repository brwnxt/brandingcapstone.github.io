About SisDec

Facial skin is the most guarded part of the body, especially for women. Sometimes we don't realize that our face is very sensitive. Damaged and sensitive facial skin is influenced by internal and external factors. So the disease that is on our facial skin varies and the problem is that people often don't know what disease they are suffering from. Here we present a mobile application by utilizing machine learning to classify types of facial skin diseases that can help the public to find out the name of the disease they are suffering from and find out the first action to be taken as well as related health information.
